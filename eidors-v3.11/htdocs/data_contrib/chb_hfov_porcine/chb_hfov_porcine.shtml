Contributed EIT Data:

Authors: Wolf GK, Grychtol B, Thompson JE, Arnold JH
Date Submitted: 14/07/2008
Date Recorded: 16/05/2005
Brief Description: Animal model of lung injury. EIT recording of HFOV ventilation. Provided are: 30s reference measurement CHB_pig06-ref.* with conventional ventilation prior to injury and a 60s recording CHB_pig06-55.* of high frequency ventilation at a MAP of 40. Ventilator oscillates at 10Hz.

Data were published in:
Wolf, G. K., Grychtol, B., Frerichs, I., Zurakowski, D., & Arnold, J. H. Regional lung volume changes during high-frequency oscillatory ventilation Pediatric critical care medicine 11(5):610-5, 2010.

Format: *.get, *.ipr, and *.prl files as generated by the GoeMF II device.
Methods: Pig torso. Single plane of 16 Electrodes numbered clockwise, with electrode #1 at the sternum. Contrary to the information in the *.prl file, the frame rate was set at 44Hz. There is no guarantee the remaining information in the *.prl file is correct.
